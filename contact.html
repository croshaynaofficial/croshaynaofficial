Frequently Asked Questions

How did you decide on the prices?
Prices for each item are dependent on the price of the materials to make the item, plus the time taken to make each item.
How quickly would I be able to get one of these shipped to me?
With the amount of time it takes to create each item, it would take 1 to 2 weeks to be created and delivered to your home!
What are the products made out of?
We use A Pound of Love yarn for worsted weight pieces, Lions Brand for the regular chunky parts, and Parfait Chunky Yarn for fluffy pieces.
