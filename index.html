WHO ARE WE?

Cro-Shayna is a small fiber arts business. We provide many products and services, including selling of crocheted items including toys, hats, shirts, pants, keychains, and more. We also sell patterns for people to buy to create their own crocheted products. We provide FREE tutorials to allow our customers to learn the art of crocheting, through small, short videos allowing even the least tech savy users to learn how to crochet. Our costs for our items and services are reasonably priced, with most being low priced, and some, including our tutorials, being FREE! Our products are made from the heart, and we'd love for you to join the Cro-Shayna family.

WE PROVIDE TUTORIALS!

Crochet is a growing hobby. Many people are now learning how to do it. Our business provides pdfs of patterns of how to make certain pieces. We also provide free tutorials on how to follow these patterns and how to crochet and knit for complete begineers. We also have videos provided on our YouTube page (also found on our website) to follow along with while you create your very own art out of yarn.
