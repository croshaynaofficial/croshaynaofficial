Chunky Purple Beanie

Cro-Shayna is a small fiber/crotching business. We provide many products and services, including selling of crocheted items including toys, hats, shirts, pants, keychains, and more. We also sell patterns for people to buy to create their own crocheted products. We provide FREE tutorials to allow our customers to learn the art of crocheting, through small, short videos allowing even the least tech savy users to learn how to crochet. Our costs for our items and services are reasonably priced, with most being low priced, and some, including our tutorials, being FREE! Our products are made from the heart, and we'd love for you to join the Cro-Shayna family.
