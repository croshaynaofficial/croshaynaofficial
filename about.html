Company Policies

If your product does not reach you then please inform us by reaching out to us using one of our many methods of contact and we will do our best to assist you in making sure the requested product reaches you in a timely manner. If you receive the incorrect product reach out to us and we will provide you with a method to send back the incorrect product you received and send you the correct one in its place. If the product is the correct one but does not meet the standards of what was promised, then we will provide you with a means of sending us back the incorrect one and we will send you one that does meet your standards.

Orders will be shipped within 7-10 busisness days.

Because all pieces are handmade, mistakes can happen. If the product is the correct one but does not meet the standards of what was promised, then we will provide you with a means of sending us back the incorrect one and we will send you one that does meet your standards. This will take some time though and no refunds will be available.

Care for Clothing

Treat your knitted and croched clothes gently and only handwash when necessary. Folding knits are recommended because hanging them will stretch them out overtime.

Wash Instructions

  1. Soak your clothes in lukewarm water and gently wash using soap or mild detergent.
  2. Air dry flat on a towel.
