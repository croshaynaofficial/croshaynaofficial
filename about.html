HISTORY OF CROSHAYNA

When I was 10, my grandmother, Grams (as seen to the left), taught me how to crochet. She was always such a nice person and always had some of greatest stories when we were crocheting together. Anytime I crochet and create my art, I think of her and how much I love her. I love sharing my craft and teaching other people how to do it. That is why we have partnered with Ashley Lillies to provide free tutorials on how to knit and crochet.
